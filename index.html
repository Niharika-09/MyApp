Shivaprasada Construction and Earth Movers

Overview

ShivaPrasada Construction and Earthmovers was established in 2010 and has adapted completely into the construction and architectural environment activities.

With over a decade of service in activities ranging from General Contracting, Turnkey Construction Projects, Roadway Works to construction equipment leasing we have grown with rich experience in the associated field.

ShivaPrasada was initially started as construction equipment leasing company with services such as transportation and earthmoving equipment and vehicles.

The company currently provides its clients with the following services

  • General Contracting
  • Road and Pathway works
  • Turnkey construction projects
  • Design, Consultation and Build
